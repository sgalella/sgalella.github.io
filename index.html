About me

My name is Santiago Galella. I have a bachelor's degree in Biomedical Engineering and a master's degree in Intelligent Interactive Systems at Pompeu Fabra University, Barcelona.

As an undergraduate student, I discovered the fascinating world computational neuroscience and neural networks. My bachelor's thesis dealt with the study of a biological neural network. While working on my thesis, I wondered about the infinite possibilities of artificial neural networks: could we achieve artificial intelligence (AI) by mimicking how biological neurons actually work?

To learn more about AI, I pursued a master focused on intelligent systems and machine learning. My master's thesis combined insights from computational neuroscience, recurrent neural networks and reinforcement learning to model decision-making under uncertainty. Currently, my research interests are focused on neural networks and reinforcement learning.

For more information about my professional career and certifications, please check out my LinkedIn profile.
